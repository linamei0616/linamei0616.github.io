About

I am a curious, independent, empathetic and analytical student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. With my burning passion of creating, I hope to create innovation changes that can better the world.

History

I was 6 when I first touched a computer. I was so fascinated but also confused with the technological world. Both my parents immigrated to America with no familiarity in English. Fast forward to high school, I received an amazing opportunity to enroll in a pre-engineering curriculum for 4 years during high school. I then discovered a passion for computer science from the rush of adrenaline at tackling code. I decided to pursue Computer Science and will be graduating in May 2025 as the first person to finish college in my family!
